Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Most branding projects take between 4 to 6 weeks from kick-off to final delivery. UI/UX projects vary based on complexity, but usually range from 6 to 12 weeks.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, for projects over $1,000, we typically split payments into a 50% deposit and 50% on completion. Custom retainers are billed monthly upfront.

What files will I receive at the end?

You will receive all industry-standard formats including AI, EPS, SVG for vectors, and high-res PNG/JPG for web use. For UI projects, you get full Figma access and assets.
